Change Timeline

May 11
Starting point: 164fps cap, C-States disabled, Process Lasso unconfigured. Two stutter types identified via CapFrameX: Type A (GPU burst, prev frame GPU 29–35ms) and Type B (CPU preemption, sim thread stalled 15–27ms).
May 11
Video mem swap high-res cars disabled. Eliminated dynamic car texture streaming causing GPU bursts when cars entered view.
May 11
MSAA 4x→2x, Crowds Off. Reduced GPU draw call load at complex corners. Partially resolved Type A spikes.
May 11
app.ini — carPreloadAll=1, trackTexturePreload=1, streamingTextureSize=256. Reduces mid-session asset streaming bursts.
May 12
Refresh rate corrected 120Hz→240Hz. Samsung LS32BG652 supports 240Hz. Running below max caused present timing mismatches with 164fps cap.
May 12
NVIDIA driver updated to 596.49, NVidia Reflex → Off, NVIDIA Control Panel configured: Low Latency Ultra, Power Management Max, Threaded Optimization On, Shader Cache Unlimited.
May 13
AMD Ryzen Balanced power plan created manually. GUID: c584c850-222a-4f65-ae2c-fe6e5b7a0c40. Set in Process Lasso power profile.
May 13
Process Lasso configured: iRacingSim64DX11 High CPU/IO, excluded from ProBalance. CapFrameX and iOverlay excluded from ProBalance. Discord pinned to odd cores at Below Normal priority.
May 13
LatencyMon finding: CPU 0 handling 473,907 ISRs and 43.2s DPC work per 6-minute session. nvlddmkm.sys DPCs up to 1,543µs. iRacing sim thread hardcoded to CPU 0 — NVIDIA interrupts competing directly.
May 13
GeForce Experience uninstalled. nvcontainer.exe adding DirectX hooks and background DPC load.
May 13
Windows Defender exclusions added for iRacing install and Documents\iRacing.
May 13
Background services DISABLED before sessions (wuauserv, UsoSvc, WSearch restart themselves if only stopped). Hard pagefaults dropped from 70,000+ to ~2,000 when properly disabled.
May 13
NVIDIA interrupt affinity root cause: RTX 4090 runs in MSI (not MSI-X) mode. Windows 11 overrides LineBased MSI affinity settings. Three device instances — Windows switches between them on reboot. Registry fix and GoInterruptPolicy both unreliable.
May 13
RTSS (RivaTuner Statistics Server) discovered running as CapFrameX overlay dependency. Caused 49,517–53,904 hard pagefaults per session and elevated dxgkrnl.sys DPCs. Directly responsible for 3 spikes in Cap21.
May 14
RTSS uninstalled. CapFrameX runs without overlay — JSON capture still works. iOverlay confirmed working independently without RTSS.
May 14
Current state: CapFrameX clean — zero spikes, worst 10.71ms (Cap24). LatencyMon shows elevated CPU 0 DPC load but CapFrameX not showing corresponding spikes. Next: AutoGpuAffinity, iOverlay isolation test.
